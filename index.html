Bhakti Geet

Mantra

Gayatri Mantra

Om bhurbhuvah swaha, tatsaviturr varenyam Bhargo devasya dhimahi, dhiyo yo nah prachodayat Hey parmeshwar hai prabhu Nirmal buddhi pradaan karo Avgunn saare door karo Tejwaan gunwaan karo Om tatpurusaya vidmaha vakratundaay dheemahi Tanno dantah prachodayat Hey ganpati mujhko riddhi do Lambodar mujhko siddhi do Vighna naa aaye kaam mein Shubh aur laabh ki vriddhi do Om sarasvatya-ch-vidmaha bramhaputrayi dheemahi Tanno vaani prachodayaat Saraswati maa kripa karo Vidya do aur gyaan do Karo pareeksha mein saphal Mujhe sukhad parinaam do Om mahalakhsamaya dheemahi vishrupiraay dheemahi Tanno lakshmi prachodayaat Hey maa lakshmi, maa yahi tumse hai meri praarthana Dhann vaibhav dekar mujhe nirdhanta sa taarna Om anjanaye vidmaha vaayuputraay dhimahi Tanno hanumat prachidayaat Dukh bhanjan hanuman ji Mujhko apni bhakti do Karm-dosh shani-dosh se mukti do prabhu mukti do.

Gayatri Mantra

Om Gan Ganpataye Namo Namah | Shri Sidhivinayak Namo Namah | Ashtakvinayak Namo Namah | Ganpati Bappa Morya
